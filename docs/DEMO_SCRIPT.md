# Demo script

A tight product demo that shows Validifier's value in under 90 seconds. The
recording uses the **public sample report** (`/sample-report`) — never a live
account. This avoids spending credits and never exposes real user data.

## Truth constraints

Every number and claim in narration must match [`src/lib/productFacts.ts`](../src/lib/productFacts.ts):

- 15 sections per report.
- Generation takes about 2–3 minutes.
- Free: 15 credits/month.
- Pro: $29/month with a 7-day free trial, 100 credits/month.
- Standard report costs 5 credits, Premium 12, chat 1.

Do not claim: guaranteed validation accuracy, guaranteed business outcomes,
specific number of paying customers, or any comparison against a named
competitor.

| Time | Screen | Narration |

| 1:25–1:30 | Cursor rests on **Start free** CTA | "Start free at validifier dot com." |

### B-roll suggestions

- Slow zooms on the score cards and competitor grid.
- A one-frame flash of the "Ready in about 2–3 minutes" copy.
- No stock footage of people cheering — clashes with an analytical brand.

## Preflight checklist

- Sign out of your account so no email, credit balance, or admin nav appears.
- Load `/sample-report` in a fresh window at 1440×900.
- Set browser zoom to 100%.
- Hide bookmarks bar, extensions bar, and OS notifications.
- Enable system Do Not Disturb.
- Clear the browser tab title to just "Validifier".
- Confirm the theme (light or dark) is intentional.

## Recording setup

- **Screen recorder**: any lossless option (macOS QuickTime, Loom desktop
  local export, ScreenStudio). Record at 60fps if possible.
- **Audio**: separate mic track; normalize to -14 LUFS integrated.
- **Cursor**: highlight and click-flash on. Keyboard-only where possible.

## Export specs

- 1920×1080, MP4 (H.264), CRF 20.
- Duration: ≤ 90 seconds for the main cut.
- No autoplay audio on landing embed — export a silent looping MP4 for the
  above-fold and keep the narrated version behind a play button.
