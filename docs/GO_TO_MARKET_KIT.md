# Go-to-market kit

All copy below is a starting point. Every reference to metrics, timing, or
pricing is grounded in [`src/lib/productFacts.ts`](../src/lib/productFacts.ts).
Do not swap in fabricated testimonials, customer counts, or outcome claims.

## Positioning

> **For** solo founders and product leads considering a new idea,
> **Validifier** turns a rough idea into a 15-section go / no-go report in
> about 2–3 minutes,
> **unlike** open-ended AI chat or blank-page validation frameworks,
> **because** it produces an opinionated, defensible structure with a
> viability score, competitor read, financial basics, and a 30-day plan.

## ICP

- Solo founders, indie hackers, product managers, and small-agency
  strategists who are actively deciding whether to build something in the
  next 30 days.
- Comfortable with SaaS ($29/mo is not a barrier).
- Prefer written analysis over long videos.

## Problem / alternatives / differentiators

| Problem | Common alternatives | How Validifier is different |
|---|---|---|
| "Is this idea worth building?" | Open-ended ChatGPT chats | Structured 15-section report with a viability score. |
| "What does the competition look like?" | Manual Googling | Dedicated competitor section inside the report. |
| "What's a realistic P&L?" | Blank spreadsheet | Financial basics section with reasoning, not just numbers. |
| "What do I do next?" | To-do lists | Built-in 30-day action plan section. |

## Founder-led 20-account outreach process

1. Build a list of 20 named humans (not companies) who match the ICP.
2. For each, note the specific idea/context they've discussed publicly.
3. Send a personal message in their platform of choice (see sequence below).
4. Track replies in a private tracker; do not add anyone to a mailing list
   without their consent.

## Objection handling

- **"Isn't this just ChatGPT?"** — Structured 15-section output, credit
  model, sample link, and Cora follow-ups. Show, don't argue.
- **"How accurate is it?"** — It's an opinionated analysis, not a
  guarantee. Point at the report's built-in risk section and encourage
  users to argue with it via Cora.
- **"Why $29 and not free?"** — Report generation and follow-ups cost
  real money to run; the Free plan (15 credits/mo, one full report)
  exists specifically so people can try before paying.
- **"What about my data?"** — Point to `/security` and `/privacy`.

## FAQ

- **What do I get on Free?** 15 credits per month. A Standard report is 5
  credits, so one full report plus 10 chat follow-ups.
- **What does Pro include?** $29/month, 7-day free trial, 100 credits per
  month, access to Premium reports (12 credits each).
- **Can I cancel?** Yes, any time from Settings. Cancellation stops the
  next renewal; you keep access through the end of the paid period.
- **Do you keep my idea?** Stored in your account and used to generate
  your reports. See `/privacy` for detail.
- **Do you sell my data?** No.
- **Is there a refund?** Contact support@validifier.com with your account
  email — see the current Terms and refund practice.

has reviewed the final draft.
